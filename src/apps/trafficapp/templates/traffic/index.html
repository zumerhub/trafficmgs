Why Traffic Alerts Matter

Traffic congestion isn't just an inconvenience; it affects productivity, fuel consumption, and even road safety. By staying informed about road closures, accidents, weather conditions, and alternative routes, drivers can save time, reduce stress, and make smarter travel decisions.

Signing up is easy! Click the "Register" button in the top navigation bar and follow the simple steps to create your account. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a password. Once registered, you can customize your profile and set up your notification preferences.

We offer a free basic plan that includes access to traffic updates and limited map features. For premium features such as personalized alerts, ad-free experience, and route planning, we offer affordable subscription plans starting at $4.99/month. To get the most out of our traffic alert service, you'll need to enable location services on your device and set up your common routes in your profile. This helps us provide you with relevant alerts for your daily commute. You can also customize notification settings to receive alerts via push notifications, email, or SMS.
